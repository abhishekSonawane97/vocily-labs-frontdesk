## Page 2 — The Problem

### The problem isn't testing. It's communication.

Your lab does the hard part well — accurate tests, fast turnaround, trusted results.

But everything a patient does *before* and *after* the test runs through one place: **the phone
at your front desk.** One person, one line at a time. They can't be on call #3 while they're on
call #2. They can't pick up at 11 PM. And every call they miss is a patient who simply dials the
next lab.

**Four questions. All day. Every day.**

> 💰 *"What's the price of a thyroid test?"*
> 📅 *"I want to book a test / home collection."*
> 📄 *"Is my report ready?"*

# Appendix (internal — NOT a deck slide)

## A1. Anonymization rules
- The lab is always **"a busy ~600-review pathology lab in Aurangabad."** Never the seed lab's real
  name. Chat-bubble brand = the fictional **"City Pathology Lab."**
- Patient names in chats (Abhishek, Priya) are illustrative placeholders, consistent with the live
  demo's seed data but not real patients.

## A2. ROI is illustrative — not a claim
- Inputs are **ranges from market research**, shown as ranges, never as a guaranteed outcome:
  - Calls/day ≈ 40–80 (a lab with hundreds of reviews is busy).
  - Missed share ≈ 15–30% (line busy + after-hours).
  - Avg test value ≈ ₹300–1,500.
- The "₹40,000–60,000/mo recovered" figure is a **mid-range illustration** (e.g. ~60 calls/day ×
  ~20% missed → ~12 missed/day; recovering ~2–3 at ~₹700 ≈ ₹42,000–63,000/mo). Every deck instance
  is labeled *illustrative* with the line *"we calculate it live with your real numbers."*
- Source: `DIAGNOSTIC-LABS-DEMO.md` §4 (impact model) and §6d (verified anchors).

## A3. Demo vs production (honesty)
- **Report delivery:** the free WAHA demo sends a **status alert** ("your report is ready"). The
  actual **PDF-in-chat** is the **production** path (Meta WhatsApp Cloud API) — the deck states this
  explicitly on page 8 and never shows a fabricated PDF-in-chat screenshot.
- **Voice receptionist** (Premium tier) is framed as *"when ready"* — it is gated on a Marathi
  latency test and is not yet built.

## A4. Pricing provenance
- Tiers and anchors from `DIAGNOSTIC-LABS-DEMO.md` §6d: Wedge ₹1,499 (₹999 intro) / Growth
  ₹3,499–4,999 / Premium ₹9,999+; receptionist anchor ₹14,000–18,000/mo; COGS ₹150–400/mo.
- **Launch offers (deck page 12):** Growth shows list ₹3,499–4,999/mo, sold at **₹1,499/mo for the
  first 10 labs**; Premium shows list ₹9,999+/mo, sold at a **discounted ₹6,000/mo** — anchor-and-
  discount launch play. (Note: Growth's ₹1,499 equals the Wedge list price, so during the launch
  Growth is effectively the only sensible entry buy.)
- Deck always carries *"final pricing confirmed on signup"* — Meta utility/marketing rates (+18%
  GST) must be re-verified before any contract.

## A5. What is NOT in the deck (deliberately)
No fabricated metrics, no testimonials/quotes, no logos, no NABL/accreditation claims, no named
client. The story sells the *capability we genuinely built and can demonstrate live* — nothing more.
